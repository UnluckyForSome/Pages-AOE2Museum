AoE2 Scenario Archive

For decades, the Age of Empires II community has crafted thousands of unique custom scenarios. Sadly, many of these have vanished—lost across old forums, broken links, and forgotten websites.

This project aims to be a community-driven effort to preserve as many creations as possible, ensuring they remain accessible and playable for years to come. Have fun browsing and using this archive :-)

Got scenarios saved somewhere? Contribute them. Every upload helps safeguard a piece of the game's creative legacy!
