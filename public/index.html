AoE2 Museum

A small collection of Age of Empires II tools and community archives. No accounts, no tracking; most tools run entirely in your browser.

Pick an exhibit.

McMinimap Render isometric minimap PNGs from recorded games and scenarios. Runs the AOE2-McMinimap Python renderer in your browser via Pyodide. Live Scenarios A community archive of Age of Empires II custom scenarios — browse, filter and download thousands of maps, and contribute your own saves. Live Unit GIFs Render animated GIFs of any AoE2 unit, action and facing straight from the original .slp graphics — decoded in your browser with a pure-JS parser and gifenc. Live
