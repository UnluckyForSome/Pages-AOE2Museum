Custom Scenario Archive

Browse, download, and contribute to a community archive of custom Age of Empires II scenarios.

For decades, the Age of Empires II community has crafted thousands of unique custom scenarios. Sadly, many have vanished—lost across old forums, broken links, and forgotten websites.

AOE2Museum aims to preserve as many creations as we can, ensuring they remain accessible and playable for years to come. As far as we know, this is the largest collection of AoE2 scenarios on the web.

Got scenarios saved somewhere? Open Contribute to upload .scn, .scx, or .aoe2scenario files (verified account required). The Analyse tab runs the museum parser and minimap renderer entirely in your browser.
