AoE2 Scenarios

For decades, the Age of Empires II community has crafted thousands of unique custom scenarios. Sadly, many of these have vanished—lost across old forums, broken links, and forgotten websites.

The AOE2Museum aims to preserve as many creations as we can, ensuring they remain accessible and playable for years to come. As far as we know, this is the largest collection of AoE2 scenarios on the web. Have fun!

Got some scenarios saved somewhere? Please Contribute them. Every upload helps safeguard a piece of the game's creative legacy!
