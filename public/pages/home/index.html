AoE2 Museum

Welcome to the AoE2 Museum! This site aims to be the home and archive for as much classic content as we can scrape together. Over the years we noticed bits starting to go missing and lost forever, so we’ve been collecting and archiving as much classic Age of Empires 2 content as we can!

Alongside this we’ve created some fun Museum Exhibits for you to try out. Have fun! You may just find a Holy Relic or two…
