AoE2 Museum

A small collection of Age of Empires II tools and community archives. No accounts, no tracking; most tools run entirely in your browser.

Pick an exhibit.

McMinimap

Render isometric minimap PNGs from recorded games and scenarios. Runs the AOE2-McMinimap Python renderer in your browser via Pyodide.
Live

Original Mods

A visual gallery of the “Original” AoE2 DE workshop series by UnluckyForSome — thumbnails and tags from mods.aoe2.se via the museum mods API.
Live

Scenarios

A community archive of Age of Empires II custom scenarios — browse, filter and download thousands of maps, and contribute your own saves.
Live

Unit GIFs

Render animated GIFs of any AoE2 unit, action and facing straight from the original .slp graphics or Definitive Edition .sld sprites — both decoded in your browser with a pure-JS parser and gifenc.
Live

Campaign Manager

Unpack and pack Genie engine campaign archives — .cpn, .cpx, .aoecpn and .aoe2campaign. Pure-JS port of withmorten/rge_campaign; files never leave your browser.
Live
