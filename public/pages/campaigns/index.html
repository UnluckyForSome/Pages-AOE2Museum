Campaign Archive

Browse campaigns, extract scenarios from your own campaign file, or pack scenarios into a new campaign—all in your browser.

Browse lists community campaigns uploaded to the museum. Upload accepts .cpn, .cpx, .aoecpn, and .aoe2campaign packages (verified account and Turnstile required). Mirrored scenarios also appear in the main scenario archive.

Extract reads campaign archives in your browser and lets you download individual scenarios or a zip. Pack builds a new campaign from ordered scenario files, compatible with withmorten’s rge_campaign tool. Local tools never upload your files unless you use Upload.
